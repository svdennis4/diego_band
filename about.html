ABOUT US

Los Hijos de Machepa is a Dominican band made up of 4 young men, each one being the son of musicians that have changed Dominican music history. The band was formed in early 2022 and since then they have experimented with a variety of genres of music including funk, jazz, rock, and Afro-Dominican folk music. Despite being influenced by their parents, the band's style of music is unique to each of their personalities. The band is expected to release their debut album in the summer of 2023.
